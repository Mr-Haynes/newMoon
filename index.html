Happy New Moon

PSALM 81:3-4

3Blow up the trumpet in the new moon, in the time appointed, on our solemn feast day.  4For this was a statute for Israel, and a law of the God of Jacob.



SIRACH 43:7

7From the moon is the sign of feasts, a light that decreaseth in her perfection.



NUMBERS 10:10

10Also in the day of your gladness, and in your solemn days, and in the beginnings of your months, ye shall blow with the tumpets over your burnt offerings, and over the sacrifices of your peace offereings; that they may be to you for a memorial before your God: I am the LORD your God.
